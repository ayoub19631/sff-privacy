Privacy Policy  ·  سياسة الخصوصية

Last updated: July 10, 2026

SFF Factory Management System is operated by Ayoub Apps for SFF Food Industries LLC. This policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information.

1. Information We Collect

  • Business data: invoices, inventory, production orders, and purchase records you enter.
  • Employee data: names, salaries, and leave requests entered by authorized HR users.
  • Login credentials: username and password for system access.
  • Device info: basic device data for offline sync only.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To operate all factory management features of the App.
  • To sync data with the Supabase cloud database.
  • To generate financial reports, inventory summaries, and production statistics.
  • To support offline mode and data recovery.

We do not sell, rent, or share your data with third parties for marketing or advertising.

3. Data Storage & Security

  • Cloud data is stored on Supabase servers with row-level security (RLS).
  • Local offline data is stored encrypted on your Windows device.
  • Access is protected by role-based authentication.
  • Service credentials are stored locally and never transmitted in plain text.

4. Data Sharing

Your data is accessible only to authorized users within your organization. We use Supabase solely to operate the App. No data is shared with advertising or analytics platforms.

5. Data Retention

Business data is retained as long as your organization uses the App. You may request deletion at any time by contacting us.

6. Children's Privacy

This App is for business use only and is not intended for users under 18. We do not knowingly collect data from minors.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Changes will appear on this page with an updated date. Continued use of the App constitutes acceptance.
